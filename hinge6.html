The hinges of love

Cheeerrs to Pete and his husband! and to all the important achievements Hinge got for "focusing in their customers and their mission" in a way that seemed honest. But I am sure you are all thinking what happened to Kate? In February 2015 Justin was travelling to London for the launching of Hinge so he sent Kate an email asking if they could meet. Kate had moved out to Zurich and was soon to marry someone. Justin figured out where she lived so he took a plane and showed in Zurich by surprise. They met up and ended up having an 8 hour coffee together.

But what happened next?


  • Kate called off the weeding and moved into Justin's studio in NYC the next week


  • Justin's and Kate's love story got turned into a film


  • Match.com bought out Hinge and Justin received the biggest sum of money he has ever seen in his live.
